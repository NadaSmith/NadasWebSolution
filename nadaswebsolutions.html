Do you provide web hosting and domain registration services?
We do not directly provide web hosting and domain registration services. Instead, we advise our clients to sign up for Squarespace hosting and domain registration themselves. Through a detailed Loom video tutorial, we guide clients step-by-step through the setup process, empowering them to take control of their online presence. We offer ongoing support and assistance to address any questions or concerns during the setup process, ensuring a smooth transition to managing their website independently. However, if preferred, we can also take care of hosting and domain registration services for our clients.
Do you provide website maintenance services?
Yes, I provide ongoing website maintenance services to keep your website up-to-date, secure, and running smoothly. Maintenance services may include updates, backups, security checks, and technical support.
Can you design a mobile-responsive website?
Absolutely! I specialize in creating mobile-responsive websites that adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and devices, providing an optimal viewing experience for all users.
Will my website be search engine friendly?
Yes, I implement SEO best practices during website development to ensure that your website is optimized for search engines. This includes proper structuring, meta tags, site speed optimization, and more.
Can you integrate third-party services or platforms into my website?
Yes, we have experience integrating various third-party services and platforms such as payment gateways, social media APIs, email marketing tools, and more. We can seamlessly integrate these services into your website to enhance its functionality and user experience.
